Is your home unbearably hot with no AC?

This was the problem faced by boy genius Billy Gonzalez, who was raised in a humble community where 95% of residents couldn’t afford to install expensive AC.

“I knew my family wasn’t the only who were suffering with the heat. All my friends at school used to complain about how unbearably hot it was at home during the summer.”

After being offered a scholarship to Ivy league university's with his revolutionary device, it all came down to when Billy was sitting in his uncomfortably hot bedroom when suddenly a lightbulb went off in his head.

“I thought, 'why don't I just make an AC for myself.' So I did, I made myself a portable AC.”

Months later when the prototype was done, it worked so well that he made another for his grandma who lived in the same community. 

Soon after, everyone in his neighborhood was begging him to make one for them. It was an instant hit. 

But it didn't stop there...

Billy began to further develop the device to also work as a humidifier, air purifier and regular fan rolled into one. 

It has been marked as the most affordable and effective air conditioning device on the market - so it wasn't long before angel investors rushed to bring the product to market.

“It was then I knew I was on to something big that could help so many. Something that could help everyone who doesn’t want the huge expense of installing a costly AC unit.” - Billy said.

Billy’s portable air conditioner has been a huge success, and has brought sweet relief to many families who suffer during the summer months. It’s small enough to place discreetly in the corner of a room, and can plug in anywhere to start providing ultimate chilled air instantly.

It's also more than 2x as efficient and cost effective as a regular AC, so you save more money while you cool off faster. And, it works so well that after a few minutes you may actually want to go outside to warm up.

"It's being called one of the greatest accomplishments in electrical engineering of the last 30 years."

 It fits perfectly on a desk, kitchen or bedroom table and it will instantly cool down the entire space in minutes!

We decided to give Billy’s portable AC a try for ourselves before recommending it, and once we turned it on in the office, it was so cold that everyone wanted to one. It’s such an amazing accomplishment to create so much cold air from such a small box. It's no surprise that scholarships were offered to this future tech wizard.
